Description
TopsRecycling is the industry leader for waste off-the-road (OTR) tire recycling in China. Founded in 2001, my company provides complete tire recycling machines and services to the mining industry and waste tyre recyclers in China. TopsRecycling has developed equipment and systems to handle all sizes of OTR tires, no matter how big size, and specialized in the recycling of giant OTR mining tires up to the size of 59/80R63.
With years of experience and commitment to handle large volumes of giant OTRs, TopsRecycling has realized the market demand for a reliable tire recycling equipment to handle all of the scrap tires produced by today’s mining companies. By utilizing a variety of specialty equipment, TopsRecycling’s waste tyre recycling machine can turn scrap OTRs into a variety of valuable products, including:end-of-life off-the-road (OTR) tires recycling field in the world soon. When the end-of-life off-the-road (OTR) tires is cut into those three pieces, it will be much easier for the waste tyre collector to pick up and transport. Or it can be cut into rubber chips on site by our waste OTR tyre strip cutter & block cutter.
