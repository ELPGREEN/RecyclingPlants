Waste Tyre Recycling Equipment

This Waste Tyre Cutting machine is used to cut waste tyre into two halves on its circumference in waste tyre recycling factory. When the waste tyres are cut into halves, it will be easier for the next step of waste tyre strip cutting and block cutting.

Advantages of this machine

  1. High efficiency (it can cut up to 80 pcs of tyres per hour if the labor works hard).
  2. It is quite safe and friendly to the user. In the cutting process, the operator is far from the cutting blades.
  3. The running cost is very low (about 2 kW per hour).
  4. The blade maintenance is very convenient, when the blade gets blunt, just grind it in situ on the machine without dismantling them off.
  5. It is useful for waste tyre recycler (if cut tyres into two halves on this machine, it will be much easier for the tyre strip cutting) and waste tyre trader (it will be easier for transportation. When the tyres are cut into two halves, they can be overlapped like bows or plates, so, the container or truck can load more tyres).

FAQ

Q: What type of tyres can be cut on this machine?
A: It is very efficient to cut any size of truck and bus tyres (both steel radial tyre and nylon tyres). It can also cut passenger tyres, but the capacity will be decreased, because the tyre size is small, it can be cut off very soon. And takes more time for tyre loading & unloading. So, we don't recommend cutting passenger tyres on this machine.
Q: How many tyres can it cut per hour?
A: It depends on the tyre size and work efficiency of the labor. If the labor works harder, the efficiency will be higher.
Q: What's the electric power consumption?
A: In general, about 2 kW per hour to cut about 60 pcs of tyres.
Q: How many labors required to operate this machine?
A: One operator only. However, if the tyre source is far from the machine site, it needs another person to transport the tyres to nearby the machine.
Q: What's the lifespan of the cutting blade?
A: It can cut about 20000 pcs of tyres.
Q: How can the cutting blade be sharpened when it gets blunt?
A: The blade can be sharpened by manually grinding machine in situ on the machine itself. No need to dismantle it from the machine and bring it to the processing center. So, it is very convenient and cheap.
Q: What's the maintenance of this machine?
  1. Keep the blade sharp always, if it gets blunt, grind it in situ on the machine by hand grinder.
  2. Lubricate the bearings regularly.
  3. Keep the hydraulic oil clean, replace it as per the operation manual provided.
  4. Check the screw bolts and nuts regularly. Tighten them if any of them get loose.
