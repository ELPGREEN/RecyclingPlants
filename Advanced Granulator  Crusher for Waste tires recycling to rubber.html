Introduction

The Granulator/Crusher for Waste Tires Rubber Granule System is a machine designed to efficiently grind rubber mulch from the Grater into small granules as fine as 1mm. These granules are then screened and re-circulated until the desired size is achieved. The Granulator boasts precise blade alignment and a well-thought-out design, resulting in a highly uniform cut and superior quality granules. Throughout the granulation process, 99% of the fiber content is effectively removed, leaving behind contaminant-free crumb rubber granules that are ready for further processing or sale.

Video demo Machine



The final output from this plant includes:

  • Rubber Mulch
  • Wire Pieces

These products are obtained through the processing of waste tires and are essential components for various applications, including landscaping, playgrounds, and construction materials.

Description
The ELP GREEN TECHNOLOGY Crumbler granulator series is uniquely designed to process wire-free rubber chips into small granules as fine as 2000 microns (2 mm). During this process, 99.9% of the fiber and steel is separated, leaving a contaminant-free crumb rubber granulate.
The Crumbler is your best machine for clean crumb!
Process up to 1000 kg/hr of 25 mm (1” inch) rubber chips input.
Output range is 1/4” to2000 microns (2 mm) crumb rubber.
Ideal for processors demanding the best output for crumb rubber products and applications.
