Introduction to CR-P300 Tire Recycling Plants

Welcome to the revolutionary CR-P300 Tire Recycling Plant production line. Designed to turn old tires into high-quality rubber powder, this production line offers an efficient and environmentally friendly solution for handling tire waste.

With an output capacity of 300 kg per hour, the CR-P300 provides an exceptional combination of performance and sustainability. With advanced features such as automatic separation of wire from rubber and a high fiber separation rate, this production line ensures a competitive final product in the market.

Our company is committed to providing not only high-quality equipment but also comprehensive technical support. From initial assembly to training for operation and equipment maintenance, we are here to ensure the success of your tire recycling venture.

With low energy consumption, low noise, and pollution-free operation, the CR-P300 is a smart choice for companies seeking efficiency, profitability, and environmental responsibility. We look forward to helping you start your journey towards a more sustainable and profitable production.

Discover how the CR-P300 can turn your tire waste into valuable raw material and contribute to a cleaner and more sustainable future.

Features:

  • Transformation of old tires into rubber powder.
  • Total and automatic separation of wire from rubber.
  • Tires, after processing, have their steel wires completely separated, and fiber can be separated 99% or more. The final product is high quality and competitive in the market.
  • Factory assembly and technical instruction provided by our company.
  • Low energy consumption, low noise level, and excellent cost-performance ratio.
  • High self-immunization, requiring less labor.
  • Advanced design, high yield, and long blade life of the secondary rubber crusher, which improves operational efficiency and reduces maintenance costs.
  • The line features a dust remover, ensuring a pollution-free work environment, with no fine dust to keep the area clean and no fibers flying around, meeting government environmental requirements.
  • Free Applications Manual.
  • Equipment operation and maintenance training provided.
  • Possibility of machine expansion.

Block Tire Conveyor:

Function: Transports block tires to the crusher to be cut into smaller pieces.

Rubber Powder Separator

Function: Separates rubber granules from powder, sending granules to the crusher and powder to the magnet through the belt.

Small Conveyor:

Function: Transports smaller materials for further processing.

Large Magnetic Separator:

Function: Separates larger magnetic materials.

Small Magnetic Separator:

Function: Separates smaller magnetic materials.

Small Conveyor (Screen)

Function: Screens different sizes of powder. Insufficiently crushed materials are sent back to the large conveyor crusher for secondary crushing.

Vibrating Screen:

Function: Screens different sizes of powder. Insufficiently crushed materials are sent back to the large conveyor crusher for secondary crushing.

Fiber Separator:

Function: Efficiently separates smaller fibers from rubber granules.

Tyre Strip Cutter:

Purpose: Separates the steel ring from the rubber after the initial process.

Block Cutter:

Purpose: Cuts the strip into blocks.

Metal Separator (XKP-450):

Purpose: Crushes the rubber.

Block Crusher (XKP-450):

Purpose: Crushes the rubber blocks into smaller pieces.

Space Required for Equipment:

Based on technical drawings data, it is noted that, for the equipment location, the following size is required: L * W * H: 43 meters * 8.50 meters * 5 meters. The actual size, after the machines are manufactured, may have a slight difference depending on the actual condition.

Size for the Workshop:

We suggest the following dimensions for the plant. The length should be approximately 80 meters, and the width should be around 15-20 meters, with a height of about 7 meters. Therefore, the total area will be around 1200 square meters.
