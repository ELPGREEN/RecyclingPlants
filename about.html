About us

ELP | GREEN TECHNOLOGY & TOPS Recycling Group

Welcome to ELP | GREEN TECHNOLOGY, founded in 2016 by Ericson Rodrigues Piccoli. From the outset, we have been dedicated to offering innovative and sustainable solutions to global challenges. In 2021, we strengthened our vision by forming a strategic partnership with the TOPS Recycling Group TOPS Recycling Group, led by Samuel Xu. With over two decades of excellence, TOPS is a pioneer in manufacturing tire shredding equipment.

History

In 2016, Ericson Rodrigues Piccoli founded ELP | GREEN TECHNOLOGY with a clear and passionate vision for innovative and sustainable solutions to global challenges. Since its inception, ELP has been committed to turning environmental issues into business opportunities through the use of green technologies.

With an innovative vision and a firm commitment to sustainable development, ELP quickly stood out in the market. A focus on sustainability allowed the company not only to address but also to overcome environmental challenges, becoming a catalyst for positive change.

A significant milestone in ELP's journey was its strategic partnership with a renowned industrial machinery manufacturer in China, sealed in 2021. This alliance not only expanded ELP's brand to three continents - Europe, Asia, and South America - but also boosted its global presence.

Partnership with TOPS Recycling Group

Joining forces with the TOPS Recycling Group, led by Samuel Xu, brought consolidated expertise in tire shredding equipment since 2001. Together, ELP and TOPS forged a solid partnership, capitalizing on TOPS' expertise and ELP's innovative vision.

ELP GREEN TECHNOLOGY stands out for developing exceptional machines, with high performance and industrial design, in collaboration with the TOPS Recycling Group. These machines are designed to address global challenges related to waste disposal, promoting recycling projects, reuse, and environmental management improvement.

Over the years, ELP has established itself as a company committed to sustainability and positive environmental impact. Seeking strategic partnerships and developing advanced technologies, the company attracted significant investments, building a solid reputation as an industry leader.

Goals

The goal of ELP Green Technology is to create a future where sustainability is the basis of all actions. The company seeks to implement advanced technologies, efficient waste management practices, and renewable energy sources to reduce carbon emissions and combat climate change. Additionally, ELP Green Technology aims to build a circular economy, where waste becomes a valuable resource and where the balance between economic growth and environmental preservation is achieved.

Comprehensive Sustainability
Integrating sustainability into all operations, from energy production to waste recycling.
Continuous Technological Innovation
Staying at the forefront of innovation, using advanced technologies to create eco-friendly solutions.
Responsible Global Expansion
Expanding globally ethically, promoting the sale of raw materials and tire recycling machines to contribute to the circular economy and environmental preservation.

Advanced Tire Recycling Technologies

  • Development and provision of complete production lines for tire recycling.
  • Customized equipment for tire shredding and conversion into rubber byproducts.
  • Technology for producing Tire-Derived Fuel (TDF) and other rubber-based products.

Specialized Technical Support and Training:

  • Technical assistance for equipment installation, operation, and maintenance.
  • Training for operators and maintenance staff to ensure efficient and safe operations.

Commitment to Customer Satisfaction and Custom Solution Development

  • Development of customized solutions to meet specific customer needs.
  • Dedicated customer service to address inquiries and provide timely support
  • Focus on product quality and customer satisfaction across all aspects of operations.
